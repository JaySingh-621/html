Ingredients

  • mayo
  • bread
  • cheese
  • any meat

Sandwiches are very delicious and easy to make.

The Procedure

First grab 2 slices of bread and lay them out. Put mayo on your slices and then put the meat on. Then put the cheese and anyhting else you would like. FInally you could heat it a little bit or eat it like this but i would recomend putting it on the stove a little.

I make these sandwiches all the time because of how easy and quick they are.
